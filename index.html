CH.02End-to-End Production
FLOW
WE DON'T START WITH images. WE START WITH FLOW.

Every project begins by understanding how stock moves, how ideas are formed, and how content needs to scale across channels. Then we build the pipeline that makes it happen.

Stock & Intake
+
Understanding how product moves through your system — from intake to studio — is where production efficiency is won or lost. We map stock flow before a single shot is planned.
Concept & Planning
+
Briefs, shot lists, storyboards, and pre-visualisation — we develop the creative and logistical plan together so physical production is clearer, faster, and better resourced.
Physical Production
+
Studio or location, product or lifestyle — we build the right team and setup for what the work actually needs. No default rosters, no inflated crews. Just the right people.
Output & Scale
+
Finished, formatted, and delivered across every channel. We manage the output pipeline — retouching, formatting, versioning — so content lands right, everywhere it needs to.
Hybrid by Design
+
Physical and digital production aren't separate tracks — they're one system. We design workflows that move between the two, using digital tools to inform and extend physical work.
CH.03Digital & 3D
BEFORE IT'S BUILT,
IT CAN BE DEFINED.

Digital and 3D tools allow us to explore space, form, lighting, and composition before committing to physical production — adding dimension without losing realism.

01
Define Dimension
Prototype ideas and make creative decisions earlier — so physical production is clearer and more efficient.
  • Blockouts & spatial planning
  • Composition and camera tests
  • Material + lighting studies
  • Environments → real sets
02
Reduce Risk
Digital planning reduces uncertainty, re-work, and reshoots while improving consistency at scale.
  • Faster approvals via clear previews
  • Better consistency across teams
  • Less waste in time, build, budget
  • Briefs that protect creative intent
03
Set Pre-Vis
Before a set is built, we can walk through it — layout, lighting, and props resolved in 3D first.
  • Set pre-vis & layout testing
  • Lighting and material exploration
  • Digital environments & extensions
04
3D Building Blocks
Reusable digital assets that speed up decision-making and stay useful long after the shoot.
  • Assets reusable across campaigns
  • Bridges physical and digital work
  • Faster turnaround on variations
