02 End-to-End Process
How We Work

We
Start
With
Flow.

Every engagement begins by mapping how product and content move through your business — before we plan a single shot. The right workflow upstream means less waste, fewer reshoots, and better output across every channel.

01

Map how products arrive, move, and need to be captured before planning a single shoot. Understanding stock flow reduces duplication, cuts reshoots, and ensures nothing is missed.

02

Creative briefs, shot lists, set planning, and 3D pre-vis. We define exactly what needs to be captured before the crew arrives — reducing wasted time on the day.

03

The right team, the right setup, the right brief. Photography and set build executed against a defined system rather than improvised on the day.

04

Retouching, formatting, versioning, and delivery — built into the production system from the start. Output scaled consistently across channels without rework.

05

Where AI and digital extend the physical — adding variation, scaling output, and creating assets that would otherwise require additional shoots.

AI Is
The
Amplifier.

Not the starting point. We integrate AI where it adds genuine value — extending production rather than replacing the craft, realism, and consistency that make content work.

AI production works best when it's grounded in a real production system — not used as a standalone tool.

01
Generate the AI Model

Photorealistic e-commerce model built to your brand spec — consistent pose, proportions, and lighting across every SKU.

02
Apply the Cut-Out Garment

Real product cut-out mapped directly onto the model — fabric texture, drape, and detail preserved naturally.
